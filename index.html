Visit C4V & Near Prime

Two sites, about 15 minutes apart in New York's Southern Tier, pioneering next-generation lithium-ion cell chemistry and domestic Gigafactory manufacturing. This page has everything you'll need to plan your trip.

Vestal, NY — R&D Endicott, NY — Gigafactory ~15 min between sites

C4V is a battery technology company developing proprietary cathode and cell chemistries, based at our Innovation Center in Vestal. Near Prime Technologies is our commercial manufacturing arm — operating a lithium-ion cell Gigafactory in Endicott. Most visitors tour both sites in a single half-day.
